Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study!


In this study, you will be asked to listen to someone read a children's story! The story is roughly 30 minutes long. Occasionally, the speaker will make a mistake and say the wrong word. Your job is to listen carefully and push the space tab whenever you hear an error. At the end of the story, you will type the last word that the speaker said into the text box to complete the task.


Note: You will not be able to pause the story after starting! So please give yourself 30-40 minutes of uninterrupted time!

You will start by listening to some introductory sentences which have NO ERRORS in them. This is to provide a short snippet of how the audio sounds in general (i.e. the audio quality).

Comprehension Check:

 1. What do you hear an error?



2. What do you do at the end of the study?



3. Will there be any errors in the first practice part?



4. Is your screen in Full screen mode?



5. Is your volume turned up sufficiently? (We recommend using headphones/ear buds, if available)

Then press Enter to continue!
